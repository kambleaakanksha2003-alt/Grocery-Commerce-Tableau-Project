# Grocery-Commerce-Tableau-Project

## 🚀 Project Overview

The Hyperlocal Grocery Commerce Analytics Project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Tableau.

This project analyzes order-level transactional data of a hyperlocal grocery delivery business to uncover insights across revenue, customers, products, and delivery operations.

The objective is to transform raw data into interactive dashboards that support strategic decision-making

---
## 🎯 Business Problem

Hyperlocal grocery businesses operate in a competitive, fast-paced environment where:

Revenue drivers are not clearly identified

Customer retention needs improvement

Inventory planning requires optimization

Delivery performance impacts customer satisfaction

---

## 📌 Project Objectives

Monitor revenue trends and growth patterns

Identify top-performing products and categories

Analyze customer retention and purchase behavior

Evaluate delivery efficiency and cancellation trends

Enable interactive data exploration for stakeholders

---

## 📈 Key Insights

A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue (Pareto Principle).

Repeat customers drive a large portion of business revenue.

Specific cities consistently outperform others in sales.

Faster delivery time correlates with higher customer ratings.

Certain categories show steady monthly growth.
